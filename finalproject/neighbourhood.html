First songs

In early 2012, The Neighbourhood released "Female Robbery" and "Sweater Weather".In May 2012, the band unveiled their debut self-released extended play titled I'm Sorry... for free download, produced by Justyn Pilbrow.In December 2012, The Neighbourhood released their second EP, Thank You.

Steps of the band

The band went on tour opening for Imagine Dragons on July and September 2013.The Neighbourhood also made two appearances in Eastern Canada during the summer of 2013. They played as one of the headliners of Toronto's Edgefest on July 31, 2013,and then a few days later played at Canada's largest music festival, Osheaga, in Montreal on August 4.The Neighbourhood performed at Atlanta's Music Midtown on September 21, 2013. On April 2014 The Neighbourhood announced their summer 2013 tour called The Love Collection Tour along with Lovelife, the 1975, and JMSN as well as that they were planning the release of a mixtape.
